Clans and Guilds is a team based PvP sandbox. Each server is a procedurally generated voxel-based map where players can gather or steal resources to build bases, weapons, armor, and more. To flourish, players may want to join or create a clan to work as a team with other players. Clans are player-governed teams that can lay claim to land, build bases, ally with other clans, and ultimately try to claim dominance over the server.

Key Features

We believe to bring our game's vision to reality, there are a handful of key features that make the most impact on gameplay.

Clans - It all starts with a shared goal

Bases - Build your kingdom, then protect it

Raiding - Not every clan is friendly

Crafting - Industry is the backbone of any powerful clan

Domination - There can only be one

Design Pillars

Player-governed

We believe the most raw and memorable experiences take place when players are given the reigns. Instead of imposing strict gameplay rules within clans to force fair democracy, preventing theft, or punishing teamkilling, etc, clans manage all of these things themselves. If your clan cannot stand without the help of game rules, then your clan shouldn't succeed.

Politics & Notoriety

Fame, reputation, and notoriety and all examples of what we call invisible stats. In a player-governed world, being known for betrayal and theft will (and should) affect your ability to join powerful, organized clans. Conversely, well-reputable players may thrive as leaders and beacons of trust.

Skill Reigns Supreme

Our team doesn't believe your value as an individual or a clan should be based on your level, time played, or the number of members in your clan. You aren't rewarded heavily for min maxing damage numbers or bum-rushing bases, you are rewarded for learning deep metas and effective strategies with the tools provided. It's important that a very skilled player could defeat many low-skilled players by themselves, or a small clan could successfully steal from a larger, less organized clan.

Fantasy

Nothing beats the feeling of marching into battle with your closest allies at your side. A video game match being labelled "competitive" by the game is not the same as you and your buddies raiding a clan that stole from your base last night. We strive for real competition and passion through our game's interactions.


There are long, rich histories that come from player-governed games. Anarchy Minecraft servers, games like Rust, DayZ, and ARK all have intense player interactions with real stakes on the line. Clan politics and teamwork from games like World of Warcraft or even Roblox war clans have been renowned for creating lifelong friendships. Our team was and still is inspired by those experiences. Clans and Guilds is our love letter to those stories and our attempt and making the best game based around these interactions.

We've realized that player governance, a shared space, and a shared goal is just about all you need to create that magic. We focus on the abilities of players to make any type of base they want, run their clan how they want, even behave how they want, all in the name of their own personal goals.

Do you seek the power of running a large clan? The thrill of treachery and theft? The glory of stomping a rival clan into the ground for daring to disrespect you and your friends? The pride of utilizing resources in the most effective ways possible and profitting off of the wars of others? The comradery of finding a few new friends and surviving with them? An open-world sandbox experience is the perfect battleground for the premier competitive clan game.
